Welcome to the Central Library of Chhattisgarh Swami Vivekanand Technical University, Bhilai. Our library serves as a vibrant knowledge hub, providing students, faculty, and researchers access to a wide range of academic resources. With a vast collection of books, journals, e-resources, and research materials, we support a diverse array of technical and professional fields. Committed to fostering a culture of learning and innovation, we offer state-of-the-art facilities, digital learning tools, and a quiet environment ideal for study and research.
